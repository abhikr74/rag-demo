## 1. Welcome to ACME Corporation

Welcome to ACME Corporation! We are excited to have you join our team. This handbook contains important information about your employment, our policies, procedures, and benefits.

## 2. Employment Policies

### 2.1 Equal Opportunity Employment
ACME Corporation is committed to providing equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants regardless of race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, national origin, age, disability, or veteran status.

### 2.2 Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Remote work arrangements must be documented and reviewed quarterly. All remote workers must maintain secure internet connections and follow IT security protocols.

### 2.3 Working Hours
Standard business hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Flexible working arrangements may be available depending on role requirements and business needs.

## 3. Benefits and Compensation

### 3.1 Health Insurance
ACME provides comprehensive health insurance coverage including medical, dental, and vision. Coverage begins on the first day of the month following 30 days of employment.

### 3.2 Retirement Plan
We offer a 401(k) retirement plan with company matching up to 4% of your salary. You are eligible to participate after 90 days of employment.

### 3.3 Professional Development
Each employee receives $2,000 annually for professional development activities including conferences, training courses, and certifications.

## 4. Work Environment and Policies

### 4.1 Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to maintain high standards of professional behavior, treat colleagues with respect, and act with integrity in all business dealings.

### 4.2 Safety Procedures
In case of emergency, immediately contact security at ext. 911 or dial 911 for external emergencies. Fire exits are located on each floor and evacuation procedures are posted near all exits.

## 5. IT and Security Policies

### 5.1 Password Policy
Passwords must be at least 12 characters long, contain uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Passwords must be changed every 90 days.

### 5.2 Data Security
All confidential company data must be stored on approved systems. Do not store sensitive information on personal devices or cloud services not approved by IT.

### 5.3 IT Support
For technical support, contact the IT helpdesk at it-support@acme.com or call ext. 4357. Support hours are 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

## 6. Leave and Time Off

### 6.1 Vacation Policy
Full-time employees earn 15 days of vacation in their first year, increasing to 20 days after 5 years of service. Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.

### 6.2 Sick Leave
Employees accrue 1 hour of sick leave for every 30 hours worked, up to a maximum of 40 hours per year. Sick leave can be used for personal illness or to care for family members.

### 6.3 Personal Days
All employees receive 3 personal days per year that can be used for any purpose. Personal days do not carry over to the next year.

## 7. Performance and Development

### 7.1 Performance Reviews
Annual performance reviews are conducted each year on your hire date anniversary. Mid-year check-ins are scheduled to provide ongoing feedback and support.

### 7.2 Career Development
We encourage employees to discuss career goals with their managers. Internal job postings are shared company-wide before external recruitment begins.

For questions about this handbook, please contact HR at hr@acme.com or ext. 2468.
